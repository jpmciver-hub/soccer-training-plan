# Summer 2026 Soccer Training Plan

**Player:** U11 Pre Academy | WakeFC  
**Age:** 10 years old  
**Goal:** Develop technical foundation, speed, and confidence for pre-academy level play  
**Duration:** June - August 2026 (12 weeks)

---

## Training Structure

| Day | Focus | Duration |
|-----|-------|----------|
| Monday | [Footwork & Touches](drills/footwork-and-touches.md) | 60 min |
| Tuesday | [Speed & Agility](drills/speed-and-agility.md) | 45 min |
| Wednesday | [Dribbling & 1v1](drills/dribbling-and-1v1.md) | 60 min |
| Thursday | [Light Strength & Conditioning](drills/strength-and-conditioning.md) | 30-40 min |
| Friday | [Shooting & Finishing](drills/shooting-and-finishing.md) | 60 min |
| Saturday | Game / Scrimmage / Clinic | varies |
| Sunday | Rest / Active Recovery | -- |

> Adjust schedule around WakeFC team practices and clinics. On days with team practice, shorten individual work to 20-30 min of targeted skill work (touches, juggling) before or after.

---

## Drill Plans

- [Footwork & Touches](drills/footwork-and-touches.md) - Ball mastery, sole rolls, pull-backs, foundation touches
- [Speed & Agility](drills/speed-and-agility.md) - Acceleration, change of direction, ladder work, reaction drills
- [Dribbling & 1v1](drills/dribbling-and-1v1.md) - Close control, moves, beating a defender, shielding
- [Shooting & Finishing](drills/shooting-and-finishing.md) - Striking technique, placement, volleys, driven shots
- [Defending](drills/defending.md) - Body positioning, closing down, tackling, recovery runs
- [Light Strength & Conditioning](drills/strength-and-conditioning.md) - Bodyweight strength, core, balance, light resistance

## Weekly Touch Goals

Use the [Touch Tracker app](https://jpmciver-hub.github.io/touch-tracker/) to log daily touches.

| Week Block | Daily Goal | Focus |
|------------|-----------|-------|
| Weeks 1-3 | 500 touches | Build habit, establish routine |
| Weeks 4-6 | 750 touches | Increase volume and intensity |
| Weeks 7-9 | 1,000 touches | Game-speed repetitions |
| Weeks 10-12 | 1,000+ touches | Maintain and peak for tryouts/season |

## 12-Week Progression Overview

See [weekly-plan.md](weekly-plan.md) for the detailed week-by-week plan with specific drills, sets, and reps.

| Phase | Weeks | Theme |
|-------|-------|-------|
| **Foundation** | 1-3 | Habit building, technique basics, baseline fitness |
| **Development** | 4-6 | Increased intensity, combo moves, positional work |
| **Competition** | 7-9 | Game-speed reps, pressure drills, tactical awareness |
| **Peak** | 10-12 | Sharpen strengths, maintain fitness, confidence building |
