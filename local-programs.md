# Local Programs & Clinics (Cary, NC Area)

**Summer 2026 | Supplemental Training Options for U11 Pre Academy**

> These programs supplement the daily individual training plan. The goal is 1-2 external sessions per week layered on top of WakeFC practices and individual work. Don't over-schedule -- quality rest and individual skill work matter more than stacking clinics.

---

## 1. WakeFC Pre-Academy Clinic

**Best for:** Technical skills and positional play in a structured club environment  
**Who:** Birth years 2016-2012 (U11 eligible)

WakeFC runs a summer clinic series that includes a **Pre-Academy Clinic** specifically designed for players at the pre-academy and academy level. Sessions are divided by position and/or technical skill level.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Program** | Pre-Academy Clinic (part of Summer Clinic Series) |
| **What's covered** | Position-specific training, technical skill development |
| **Other clinic options** | Finishing & Saving Clinic, Future Jrs Clinic |
| **Locations** | Middle Creek Park (Apex), Ting Park (Holly Springs), WakeMed Soccer Park (Cary) |
| **Pricing** | ~$150 for all sessions, or pick 8 sessions for ~$100 |
| **What to bring** | Ball (name/phone on it), shin guards, cleats, sunscreen, food, water |
| **Website** | [Wake FC Summer Clinics](https://www.wakefc.com/Default.aspx?tabid=1458721) |
| **Contact** | 919-249-5119 / info@wakefc.com |

### WakeFC Summer Camps (additional options)

| Camp | Focus |
|------|-------|
| Skill Accelerator | Technical skill intensives |
| Pre-Academy Camp | Preparing for pre-academy competition level |
| Pre-Academy & Academy Finishing | Scoring and shooting focus |
| Pre-Academy & Academy Goal Keeping | GK-specific |

**Camp info:** [Wake FC Summer Camps](https://www.wakefc.com/Default.aspx?tabid=1458708)

### Recommended Schedule
- **Pre-Academy Clinic:** 1x/week throughout summer (Saturday)
- **Summer Camp:** 1 week block during a lighter individual training week

## 2. Soccer Genome

**Best for:** Elite private training with game-realistic methodology  
**Location:** Nexus Training Facility, Preslyn Drive, Raleigh, NC 27616

Soccer Genome specializes in private and small-group soccer training. Since 2011, they've delivered tens of thousands of sessions. Their methodology is built on a "problem-solution" model -- presenting game-specific problems and having players find solutions, rather than isolated technical drills.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Philosophy** | Game-realistic training environments, problem-solution methodology |
| **Offerings** | Private 1-on-1 sessions, small group training, clinics, goalie training, tactical masterclasses |
| **Why it fits** | Highly individualized, can target specific weaknesses (e.g., weak foot, defensive positioning, composure under pressure) |
| **Location** | NE Raleigh (~25 min from Cary) |
| **Website** | [soccergenome.com](https://www.soccergenome.com/) |
| **Private training info** | [Private Training](https://www.soccergenome.com/private-train) |
| **Contact** | info@soccergenome.com |

### Club Partnerships
Soccer Genome partners with Franklin Wake Soccer Club (FWSC) for professionalized player development sessions. While this is a club-specific partnership, their private and small-group sessions are open to all players.

### Recommended Schedule
- **Private session:** 1x every 2 weeks (bi-weekly), focusing on whatever the individual plan reveals as the biggest growth area
- **Good for:** Weeks 4-9 when intensity increases and specific technical gaps emerge

## 3. Athletic Republic Cary

**Best for:** Soccer-specific speed, agility, and acceleration training  
**Location:** 607 Mills Park Dr, Cary, NC 27519

Athletic Republic has a soccer-specific program that trains acceleration, agility, and power using evidence-based protocols and patented equipment. They focus on improving mechanics for a faster first step, quicker direction changes, and better endurance.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Programs** | Ignition (foundational), Acceleration (advanced performance) |
| **Format** | Small-group, trainer-led |
| **What they train** | First-step speed, change of direction, sprint mechanics, stamina |
| **Why it fits** | Complements the home speed/agility drills with professional coaching and equipment |
| **Location** | Cary -- very convenient |
| **Website** | [cary.athleticrepublic.com](https://cary.athleticrepublic.com/) |
| **Soccer-specific page** | [Soccer Training](https://cary.athleticrepublic.com/sports-performance-training-cary-nc/soccer-training/) |
| **Free trial** | Available -- [Free Trial](https://cary.athleticrepublic.com/sports-performance-training-cary-nc/) |

### Recommended Schedule
- **1x/week** on Tuesday or Saturday (supplement the home speed/agility day)
- Start with their free trial to evaluate fit
- Best during **Phase 2-3** (weeks 4-9) when speed work intensifies

## 4. Parisi Speed School

**Best for:** Speed and agility fundamentals, age-appropriate athletic development  
**Location:** Apex, NC (near Cary)

Parisi specializes in youth speed training using fun modalities -- medicine balls, resistance bands, ladders, plyometrics. Their Game Time program builds good exercise habits and develops speed in an age-appropriate way.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Program** | Game Time (youth), Performance (older athletes) |
| **Methods** | Medicine balls, bands, ladders, plyometrics, Parisi speed methodology |
| **Why it fits** | Good foundational speed program for younger athletes; fun and engaging format |
| **Location** | Apex (~10 min from Cary) |
| **Website** | [parisiraleigh.com](https://parisiraleigh.com/) |
| **Also at** | [cor4fitness.com/speed-training-cary-nc](https://www.cor4fitness.com/speed-training-cary-nc/) |

### Recommended Schedule
- **Alternative to Athletic Republic** if looking for a different vibe or closer to Apex
- 1x/week during Phase 2-3

- Soccer Genome private session (schedule on a lighter day or combine with a Saturday)
