# Speed & Agility (Tuesday)

**Duration:** 45 minutes  
**Goal:** Improve acceleration, change of direction, and reactive speed  
**Equipment:** Cones (15+), agility ladder, stopwatch

> Rest is critical for speed work. Full recovery between reps ensures quality. This is not a conditioning session -- it's about training fast-twitch muscle fibers to fire.

---

## Warm-Up (10 min)

| Drill | Duration | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Light jog | 2 min | Easy pace, loosen up |
| High knees | 2 x 20 yards | Drive knees up, pump arms, stay on toes |
| Butt kicks | 2 x 20 yards | Heels to glutes, quick turnover |
| Carioca / grapevine | 2 x 20 yards each direction | Hips open, stay low |
| A-skips | 2 x 20 yards | Drive knee up, skip off ground, arm drive |
| Lateral shuffles | 2 x 20 yards each direction | Low stance, don't cross feet |

---

## Core Session (30 min)

### Block 1: Linear Speed (10 min)

| Drill | Sets x Reps | Rest | Coaching Points |
|-------|-------------|------|-----------------|
| **10-yard burst** | 4 x 2 | 30 sec | Explosive first 3 steps. Forward lean, drive arms. |
| **20-yard sprint** | 4 x 2 | 45 sec | Accelerate through 10 yards, maintain top speed to 20. |
| **Flying 20s** | 3 x 1 | 60 sec | 10-yard build-up, hit max speed for 20 yards. Focus on stride length and arm drive. |

### Block 2: Agility & Change of Direction (10 min)

| Drill | Sets x Reps | Rest | Description |
|-------|-------------|------|-------------|
| **5-10-5 Shuttle** | 4 | 45 sec | Start center, sprint 5 yards right, touch line, sprint 10 yards left, touch, sprint 5 back to start. Low hips on cuts. |
| **T-Drill** | 3 | 45 sec | Sprint 10 yards forward, shuffle 5 left, shuffle 10 right, shuffle 5 back to center, backpedal to start. |
| **Zig-zag cone sprint** | 4 | 30 sec | 6 cones in zig-zag, 3 yards apart. Sprint and cut at each cone. Plant outside foot, drive off. |

### Block 3: Agility Ladder (10 min)

| Pattern | Sets | Coaching Points |
|---------|------|-----------------|
| **2-foot in-each** | 3 | Quick feet, both feet in every box. Arms drive. |
| **Lateral 2-in** | 3 each direction | Face sideways, two feet in each box, move laterally. |
| **Ickey Shuffle** | 3 each direction | In-in-out pattern. Stay low, quick ground contacts. |
| **1-foot hop** | 2 each foot | Single-leg hops through each box. Balance and control. |
| **Ali Shuffle** | 3 | Straddle the ladder, feet in-out rapidly. |

---

## Cool-Down (5 min)

| Activity | Duration |
|----------|----------|
| Walking lap | 2 min |
| Static stretching | 3 min - hip flexors, quads, hamstrings, calves, ankles |

---

## Progression by Phase

| Phase | Adjustment |
|-------|-----------|
| **Weeks 1-3** | Learn the patterns. Focus on form over speed. Time baseline 5-10-5 and T-Drill. |
| **Weeks 4-6** | Increase speed. Add ball to zig-zag cone sprint (dribble through). Beat baseline times. |
| **Weeks 7-9** | Add reaction element: parent points direction, player reacts and sprints. Compete against the clock. |
| **Weeks 10-12** | Combine with soccer actions: sprint to ball, first touch, shot. Full game-speed agility. |

---

## Notes

- Time the 5-10-5 and T-Drill weekly to track improvement
- If legs feel heavy or form breaks down, stop and rest -- quality over quantity
- Can supplement with [Athletic Republic Cary](https://cary.athleticrepublic.com/) or [Parisi Speed School](https://parisiraleigh.com/) sessions 1x/week
