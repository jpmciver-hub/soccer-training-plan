# Shooting & Finishing (Friday)

**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Goal:** Develop clean striking technique, placement over power, and composure in front of goal  
**Equipment:** Ball (3-5 if possible), cones, goal or target (wall, net, cones as posts)

> At U11, technique and placement matter more than power. A well-placed driven shot at moderate pace beats a skied blast every time.

---

## Warm-Up (10 min)

| Drill | Duration | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Passing against wall | 3 min | 10 yards from wall. Alternate feet, focus on clean contact with inside of foot. |
| Laces strikes (stationary) | 3 min | Ball stationary. Strike with laces into wall/net. Focus on planting foot beside ball, locking ankle, hitting center of ball. |
| Juggling | 2 min | Laces and thigh only. Develop the feel for clean contact on top of foot. |
| Dynamic stretch | 2 min | Leg swings (front/back, side/side), hip circles |

---

## Core Session (40 min)

### Block 1: Striking Technique (15 min)

All shots from 12-15 yards.

| Drill | Reps | Coaching Points |
|-------|------|-----------------|
| **Driven shot (laces)** | 10 each foot | Plant foot pointing at target, 6 inches beside ball. Lock ankle. Strike through center of ball with laces. Follow through toward target. |
| **Placed shot (inside)** | 10 each foot | Open body, plant foot pointed slightly outward. Stroke ball with inside of foot to corners. Accuracy over power. |
| **Low driven (laces)** | 10 each foot | Lean slightly over ball. Strike top half of ball. Keep it on the ground. |
| **Chip / lob** | 5 each foot | Get underneath the ball. Short backswing, stab under the ball. For lifting over keeper. |

### Block 2: Finishing Scenarios (15 min)

| Drill | Reps | Setup |
|-------|------|-------|
| **Cone gate targets** | 10 | Place 4 cone gates (2 ft wide) in the corners and center of the goal. 1 point per gate hit. Track score. |
| **First touch and shoot** | 10 | Parent passes ball from side. First touch to set up, second touch is the shot. Alternate receiving from left and right. |
| **Turn and shoot** | 10 | Start with back to goal, 15 yards out. Receive pass, turn, shoot. Vary turn direction. |
| **Dribble and finish** | 8 | Start 25 yards out. Dribble at speed toward goal, shoot from 12-15 yards. Focus on composure -- slow down slightly before striking. |

### Block 3: Game-Realistic Finishing (10 min)

| Drill | Reps | Setup |
|-------|------|-------|
| **1v1 vs keeper/parent** | 6 | Start 20 yards out. Dribble at the parent (who closes down). Beat them and finish. |
| **One-touch finish** | 10 | Parent rolls/passes ball across the box. One touch finish into goal. Vary the angle and speed of the service. |
| **Volley / half-volley** | 10 | Parent tosses ball underhand from 5 yards. Let it bounce once (half-volley) or strike out of air (volley). Lock ankle, keep eye on ball. |

---

## Cool-Down (10 min)

| Activity | Duration |
|----------|----------|
| Free kicks from 20 yards | 5 min - fun, low pressure, aim for corners |
| Stretching | 5 min - quads, hip flexors, hamstrings, calves, ankles |

---

## Progression by Phase

| Phase | Adjustment |
|-------|-----------|
| **Weeks 1-3** | Stationary ball work. Build clean technique. No pressure. Track accuracy (shots on target out of 10). |
| **Weeks 4-6** | Add movement -- receiving passes, turning, dribbling in. Increase distance to 18-20 yards. |
| **Weeks 7-9** | Add time pressure and passive defender. 1v1 finishing at speed. |
| **Weeks 10-12** | Full scenarios: receive, beat defender, finish. Score competitions. Both feet required. |

---

## Accuracy Tracking

Track weekly progress:

| Metric | How to Measure |
|--------|---------------|
| Shots on target | Out of 10 strikes from 15 yards |
| Cone gate accuracy | How many corner gates hit out of 10 |
| Weak foot accuracy | Same drill, non-dominant foot only |

Goal by end of summer: 7/10 on dominant foot, 5/10 on weak foot from 15 yards.
