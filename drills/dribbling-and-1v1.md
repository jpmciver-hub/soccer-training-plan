# Dribbling & 1v1 (Wednesday)

**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Goal:** Develop close control at speed, confidence with moves, and ability to beat a defender  
**Equipment:** Ball, cones (15+), a partner/parent for 1v1 work

---

## Warm-Up (10 min)

| Drill | Duration | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Free dribble | 3 min | Dribble in a 15x15 yard box. Random direction changes, keep ball close. |
| Cone weave (close) | 3 min | 8 cones in a line, 2 yards apart. Weave through and back. Inside/outside touches. |
| Cone weave (wide) | 2 min | Same cones, 4 yards apart. Push the ball out, accelerate to next cone. |
| Fast feet on ball | 2 min | Toe taps, alternating feet, maximum speed for 20 sec, rest 10 sec. Repeat 4x. |

---

## Core Session (40 min)

### Block 1: Moves to Beat a Defender (15 min)

Practice each move stationary first, then approach a cone as the "defender."

| Move | Reps Each Side | Description |
|------|----------------|-------------|
| **Scissors** | 10 | Step over ball inside to out, push away with outside of opposite foot. Sell the fake with a body drop. |
| **Step-over** | 10 | Foot goes around the ball from outside to inside, push away with outside of same foot. |
| **Matthews cut** | 10 | Push ball with inside, quickly cut back with outside of same foot. Change of pace is the key. |
| **Body feint** | 10 | Drop shoulder one way, push ball the other with outside of foot. Exaggerate the lean. |
| **Chop (inside cut)** | 10 | At speed, chop ball behind standing leg with inside of foot, turn and accelerate. |
| **Drag-back and go** | 10 | Sole roll backward, push forward with laces and accelerate. Sell the stop. |

### Block 2: Moves at Speed Through Cones (10 min)

Set up 5 cones in a line, 5 yards apart. Approach each cone and execute a move as if it's a defender.

| Run | Move at Each Cone |
|-----|-------------------|
| Run 1 | Scissors at every cone |
| Run 2 | Step-over at every cone |
| Run 3 | Matthews cut at every cone |
| Run 4 | Alternate scissors and body feint |
| Run 5 | Player's choice -- different move at each cone |
| Run 6-8 | Repeat favorites at full game speed |

### Block 3: 1v1 Situations (15 min)

Requires a partner (parent, sibling, or training partner).

| Drill | Duration | Setup |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **1v1 to line** | 5 min | 10x15 yard grid. Attacker tries to dribble past defender to end line. Alternate roles every rep. |
| **1v1 to mini goals** | 5 min | Same grid with cone goals on each end. Winner scores, loser defends. First to 5. |
| **Gauntlet** | 5 min | 5-yard wide channel, 15 yards long. Defender starts at midpoint. Attacker must dribble past without losing ball or going out of bounds. |

> **Parent coaching:** If acting as the defender, start at 50% intensity weeks 1-3, increase to 75% by weeks 4-6, and near-full by weeks 7+. The player should feel challenged but succeed about 50% of the time.

---

## Cool-Down (10 min)

| Activity | Duration |
|----------|----------|
| Free dribble (easy) | 3 min |
| Juggling | 3 min |
| Stretching | 4 min - groin, hip flexors, quads, hamstrings, ankles |

---

## Progression by Phase

| Phase | Adjustment |
|-------|-----------|
| **Weeks 1-3** | Learn all 6 moves. Slow speed. Focus on technique and selling the move. |
| **Weeks 4-6** | Execute at 75% speed. Chain 2 moves together. Increase 1v1 defender intensity. |
| **Weeks 7-9** | Full speed. Read the defender before choosing a move. Add finishing after beating defender (dribble past, then shoot). |
| **Weeks 10-12** | Freestyle -- choose moves in real-time. 1v1 competitions with score tracking. |

---

## Key Coaching Points

- **Change of pace** beats defenders more than fancy moves -- slow down, then explode
- **First touch after the move** is critical -- push ball into space and accelerate
- **Head up** after beating the defender to find the next action (pass or shoot)
- **Both feet** -- practice every move on both sides even if one is weaker
