# 12-Week Summer Training Plan

**June - August 2026 | U11 Pre Academy | WakeFC**

> This plan assumes 5 training days per week with team practices and clinics layered on top. On days with WakeFC practice, reduce individual work to 20-30 min (juggling + one focused drill block). Rest on Sundays is non-negotiable -- recovery is where growth happens.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-3)

**Theme:** Build habit, learn technique, establish baseline  
**Daily touch goal:** 500  
**Intensity:** 60-70% -- focus on form, not speed

### Week 1: Getting Started

| Day | Session | Key Focus |
|-----|---------|-----------|
| Mon | Footwork & Touches | Learn inside-inside, inside-outside, sole rolls. Slow speed. |
| Tue | Speed & Agility | Run through ladder patterns slowly. Learn 5-10-5 shuttle form. Time baseline. |
| Wed | Dribbling & 1v1 | Learn scissors, step-over, Matthews cut. Stationary practice. |
| Thu | Strength & Conditioning | Learn all exercises with perfect form. Lower reps. Plank 20 sec. |
| Fri | Shooting & Finishing | Laces strike and inside-foot placement from 12 yards. Stationary ball only. Track accuracy /10. |
| Sat | WakeFC practice or pickup game | -- |
| Sun | Rest | Active recovery: walk, swim, stretch |

### Week 2: Building

| Day | Session | Key Focus |
|-----|---------|-----------|
| Mon | Footwork & Touches | Add pull-back turns and Cruyff turns. Moving touches through cones at walking pace. |
| Tue | Speed & Agility | Add T-drill. Ladder patterns at moderate speed. |
| Wed | Dribbling & 1v1 | Add body feint and chop. Approach cones at jogging speed. |
| Thu | Strength & Conditioning | Full exercise list at listed reps. Focus on single-leg balance. |
| Fri | Shooting & Finishing | Add first-touch-and-shoot. Receive from the side, set and strike. |
| Sat | WakeFC practice / Pre-Academy Clinic | -- |
| Sun | Rest | -- |

### Week 3: Consolidate

| Day | Session | Key Focus |
|-----|---------|-----------|
| Mon | Footwork & Touches | Combo patterns: inside-outside-sole cycle. Box touches around cone square. |
| Tue | Speed & Agility | All drills from weeks 1-2. Re-time 5-10-5 and T-drill. Compare to baseline. |
| Wed | Dribbling & 1v1 | All 6 moves at cones. Introduce 1v1 to line with parent at 50%. |
| Thu | Strength & Conditioning | Add tempo squats (3 sec down). Plank 25-30 sec. |
| Fri | Shooting & Finishing | Add turn-and-shoot. Cone gate target game -- track score. |
| Sat | WakeFC / Clinic | -- |
| Sun | Rest | -- |

**Week 3 Checkpoint:**
- [ ] Can execute all 6 dribbling moves (slowly)
- [ ] Clean laces strike technique from 12 yards
- [ ] Baseline times recorded for 5-10-5 and T-drill
- [ ] Hitting 500 touches per training day consistently
- [ ] Plank hold: 25+ seconds

---

## Phase 2: Development (Weeks 4-6)

**Theme:** Increase intensity, chain skills together, add pressure  
**Daily touch goal:** 750  
**Intensity:** 75-80%

### Week 4

| Day | Session | Progression |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| Mon | Footwork & Touches | Add "head up" challenge: call out fingers during cone dribbles. Increase speed. |
| Tue | Speed & Agility | Add ball to zig-zag sprint (dribble through). Increase ladder speed. |
| Wed | Dribbling & 1v1 | Chain 2 moves together (scissors into Matthews). 1v1 at 75% defender pressure. |
| Thu | Strength & Conditioning | Increase reps by 2-4 across all exercises. Band work every week. Eyes closed balance. |
| Fri | Shooting & Finishing | Increase distance to 16-18 yards. Dribble-and-finish at speed. Weak foot 30% of reps. |
| Sat | WakeFC / Speed training session (Athletic Republic or Parisi) | -- |
| Sun | Rest | -- |

### Week 5

| Day | Session | Progression |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| Mon | Footwork & Touches | Speed dribble focus -- time 40-yard dribble. Figure-8 at pace. |
| Tue | Speed & Agility | Reaction sprints: parent points, player reacts. Flying 20s for max speed. |
| Wed | Dribbling & 1v1 | Player's choice combo at cones. 1v1 to mini goals -- keep score. |
| Thu | Strength & Conditioning | Add lateral lunges. Single-leg RDL with eyes closed. Core circuit. |
| Fri | Shooting & Finishing | One-touch finishing from service. Volley introduction (tossed ball). |
| Sat | WakeFC / Soccer Genome private session | -- |
| Sun | Rest | -- |

### Week 6

| Day | Session | Progression |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| Mon | Footwork & Touches | Full session at 80% speed. Combo freestyle -- create own 4-move sequence. |
| Tue | Speed & Agility | Re-time 5-10-5 and T-drill. Compare to week 3. |
| Wed | Dribbling & 1v1 | Defending focus: close down, jockey, poke tackle. Then switch to attacking 1v1. |
| Thu | Strength & Conditioning | Plank 40 sec. Add single-leg hop and stick. Band monster walks. |
| Fri | Shooting & Finishing | Full circuit: receive, dribble, beat a cone, finish. Both feet. |
| Sat | WakeFC / Clinic | -- |
| Sun | Rest | -- |

**Week 6 Checkpoint:**
- [ ] Can chain 2 dribbling moves together at speed
- [ ] 5-10-5 time improved from baseline
- [ ] Hitting 750 touches per training day
- [ ] Can finish accurately from 18 yards (5/10 dominant foot)
- [ ] Plank hold: 40+ seconds
- [ ] Winning 50%+ of 1v1 situations

## Phase 3: Competition (Weeks 7-9)

**Theme:** Game speed, pressure, tactical awareness  
**Daily touch goal:** 1,000  
**Intensity:** 85-90%

### Week 7

| Day | Session | Progression |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| Mon | Footwork & Touches | All drills at game speed. Passive defender during cone work. Timed challenges. |
| Tue | Speed & Agility | Sprint-to-ball-and-play: sprint, receive, action (pass/shoot). Full reaction drills. |
| Wed | Dribbling & 1v1 | 1v1 at near-full defender intensity. Read the defender before choosing a move. Finish after beating. |
| Thu | Strength & Conditioning | Tempo on all movements. Plank 45 sec. Circuit format: 30 sec work / 15 sec rest. |
| Fri | Shooting & Finishing | 1v1 vs keeper/parent. Time-limited finishing (must shoot within 3 seconds of receiving). |
| Sat | WakeFC match day / Speed training | -- |
| Sun | Rest | -- |

### Week 8

| Day | Session | Progression |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| Mon | Footwork & Touches | Create pressure: partner tries to win ball during touch drills. Maintain composure. |
| Tue | Speed & Agility | Competition day: time every drill. Record personal bests. |
| Wed | Dribbling & 1v1 | 2v1 introduction (if extra player available). Defending: recovery runs at full speed. |
| Thu | Strength & Conditioning | Full circuit. Single-leg everything. Core with ball between feet for added challenge. |
| Fri | Shooting & Finishing | Weak foot emphasis: 50% of all reps on non-dominant foot. Half-volley and volley. |
| Sat | WakeFC / Soccer Genome session | -- |
| Sun | Rest | -- |

### Week 9

| Day | Session | Progression |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| Mon | Footwork & Touches | Combine with speed: touch sequence into 10-yard sprint. Game-realistic transitions. |
| Tue | Speed & Agility | Re-time 5-10-5, T-drill, 20-yard sprint. Compare to weeks 3 and 6. |
| Wed | Dribbling & 1v1 | Full 1v1 tournament format: first to 10 goals. Track moves used and success rate. |
| Thu | Strength & Conditioning | Peak week: highest reps, longest holds, fastest circuits. |
| Fri | Shooting & Finishing | Full match scenario: receive in midfield, dribble, beat defender, shoot. Score /10. |
| Sat | WakeFC | -- |
| Sun | Rest | -- |

**Week 9 Checkpoint:**
- [ ] Can execute moves at game speed with a defender
- [ ] 5-10-5 and T-drill personal bests
- [ ] Hitting 1,000 touches per training day
- [ ] Accurate from 18-20 yards (6/10 dominant, 4/10 weak)
- [ ] Plank: 45+ seconds
- [ ] Confident in 1v1 defending -- closing down, jockeying, poke tackling

---

## Phase 4: Peak (Weeks 10-12)

**Theme:** Sharpen, maintain, build confidence  
**Daily touch goal:** 1,000+  
**Intensity:** 80-85% (slight deload to stay fresh)

### Week 10

| Day | Session | Focus |
|-----|---------|-------|
| Mon | Footwork & Touches | Freestyle session -- player designs own routine. Showcase moves. |
| Tue | Speed & Agility | Maintain speed work. Reduce volume by 20%. Quality over quantity. |
| Wed | Dribbling & 1v1 | Fun 1v1 games. Competition with rewards. Build confidence and swagger. |
| Thu | Strength & Conditioning | Maintenance: same exercises, reduce to 2 sets each. Focus on mobility. |
| Fri | Shooting & Finishing | Accuracy day: target practice, cone gates, placement challenges. |
| Sat | WakeFC / match / clinic | -- |
| Sun | Rest | -- |

### Week 11

| Day | Session | Focus |
|-----|---------|-------|
| Mon | Footwork & Touches | Review: any weak areas to shore up? Extra reps on weakest skill. |
| Tue | Speed & Agility | Light speed maintenance. Ladder work for fun. |
| Wed | Dribbling & 1v1 | Pick 3 best moves -- polish them to automatic. Both feet. |
| Thu | Strength & Conditioning | Light session. Core and balance only. Stretching emphasis. |
| Fri | Shooting & Finishing | Confidence-building: moderate distance, high success rate. End on a positive. |
| Sat | WakeFC | -- |
| Sun | Rest | -- |

### Week 12

| Day | Session | Focus |
|-----|---------|-------|
| Mon | Footwork & Touches | Fun session. Juggling PR attempt. Celebrate improvement. |
| Tue | Speed & Agility | Final timed runs: 5-10-5, T-drill, 20-yard sprint. Record and compare all phases. |
| Wed | Dribbling & 1v1 | 1v1 showcase. Player demonstrates everything learned. |
| Thu | Light stretch and recovery | -- |
| Fri | Shooting & Finishing | Film a "highlight reel" of best shots. End the summer strong. |
| Sat | WakeFC | Ready for fall season |
| Sun | Celebrate | Review all progress, improvements, and goals hit |

**End of Summer Goals:**
- [ ] Juggling personal best (track throughout)
- [ ] 5-10-5 improvement from week 1 baseline
- [ ] 7/10 shooting accuracy (dominant foot, 15 yards)
- [ ] 5/10 shooting accuracy (weak foot, 15 yards)
- [ ] Comfortable beating a defender 1v1 with 3+ moves
- [ ] Sound defensive body shape and tackling technique
- [ ] Plank: 60 seconds
- [ ] Consistent 1,000-touch training days
- [ ] Streak record in Touch Tracker
